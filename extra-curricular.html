CURRICULUM

We provide numerous clubs in order for the students to showcase their talent and develop their self-expression. The following are the clubs that students may sign up for:

SPORTS

Servers Club

  • Stop, slide and save the ball from touching the ground! A volleyball club open to all boys and girls.

The Brown Bears

  • Keep your hands and eyes on the ball! A basketball club open to all boys and girls.

Bear Rackets Club

  • Make use of that racket! Develop your badminton skills here in the Bear Rackets

PERFORMING ARTS

Slayareal Club

  • Hiphop, folkdance, ballet, you name it! Join tryouts at Slayareal Club to perform dance productions in school events.

Drama Domain Club

  • “Are you kidding me?! Where do you think I learn all this stuff from?!” Don't be shy and let out all your male and female rage in this club, or maybe just give out your best acting skills!

FINE ARTS

Morii Club

  • A photography and videography club to enhance your skills in capturing fleeting moments.

Artsify Club

  • A club where you can express your feelings, thoughts and emotions through art!

Turn It Up! Club

  • Got a thing for singing or music? Show everyone what melodies you can make here!


PUBLIC SPEAKING

Speak Free Club

  • Is your mind always filled with interesting thoughts and you want to speak it out? Then the Speak Free Club may be the perfect club for you.

JOURNALISM

The Bear News

  • Your one and only primary newscast of the P.R.I.D.E. Academy where all the happenings of the school are talked about.

Pamayanan

  • Nagiisang balitang pang-Filipino ng P.R.I.D.E Academy. Nakatanggap na ng humigit-kumulang 25 mga parangal sa buong bansa.

ROBOTICS

RoboScicotics Club

  • Unveil your hidden coding and engineering skills in Roboscotics club!
