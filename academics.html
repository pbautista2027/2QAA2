CURRICULUM

The curriculum incorporates the usual High School curriculum with advanced lessons in special sciences such as Biology, and Chemistry and in languages like Filipino and English. This shall lead to the advancement of students in these courses and be knowledgeable about the fundamentals of scientific tools. Assessments are all conducted in-classroom and projects will only be done during class periods. Creative projects will be implemented in order for the students to develop their imagination and self-expression. Feedback of students’ performance may be known during card days wherein parents and students are encouraged to meet with the students’ homeroom advisers and spoken to about how they may improve.
