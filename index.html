About

Prestigious Responsible Independent Disciplined Experienced Academy, better known as P.R.I.D.E. Academy, holds the esteemed title of being recognized as one of the most outstanding schools in the Milky Way. Established in November 2023, The P.R.I.D.E. Academy was founded by Paolo Eli Bautista, along with Norel Jana Mangaliag and Tanya Annika Jimenez during their high school years. This institution serves as a lasting tribute to their notable contribution to our planet and to the human race.
















Mission

P.R.I.D.E. Academy is dedicated to the well-rounded development of students. To produce members of society who shall uphold their values, utilize their wisdom, and cherish their awareness.

Vision

An academy that seeks the upholding of values and exercising them in their lives. Finding balance, peace, and good work ethic through experiences and lessons. In touch with their identity as an individual and as part of a community. To contribute to the people that surround them and to stay open-minded. Be creative and find ways to utilize their skills and talents.
